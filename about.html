Who I Am

I am Paul Varghese, a web developer with a strong interest in how the web works at a fundamental level. I enjoy turning ideas into functional websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — the building blocks that every great site is made of.

I am passionate about web development and technology. What draws me to this field is the combination of logic and creativity: writing code that solves real problems while still looking good on screen.

What I Value

I enjoy building accessible and user-friendly websites. Accessibility isn't an afterthought for me — it's part of how I approach every project. A site that only works for some users isn't finished.

I pay close attention to semantic HTML structure, keyboard navigation, color contrast, and screen reader compatibility. These details matter because they determine whether a website is truly usable.

Where I'm Based

I am based in Thrissur, Kerala — a city known for its culture and growing tech community. Working from here keeps me connected to both local opportunities and the wider developer ecosystem online.

When I'm not coding, I like exploring new tools, reading documentation (yes, really), and working on side projects that help me learn something new.
